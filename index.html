My Marketing Diary

Developers have this amazing culture of open-sourcing everything. Their code, their knowledge, and even their mistakes. It's all out there for anyone. That culture is beautiful!

I don't see that happening enough in marketing. So, this is my small attempt to change that. I'll be documenting my marketing journey here. What I'm trying, what's working, and where I'm falling flat on my face.

I'm not here to teach or preach. Just dumping my thoughts as I go. If anything here happens to be useful to you, that would make me really happy! :)
